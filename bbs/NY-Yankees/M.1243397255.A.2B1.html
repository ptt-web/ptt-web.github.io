"I have to wait for a : chance." 王建民說：『他(Joe Girardi)昨天告訴我的，他不知道何時可以讓他加入輪值表當中』 對王建民來說，在兩場3A的復健賽中，能夠投出連續13局無失分的比賽，然而必須調整 成一位中繼投手是困難且令人沮喪的。 王建民說：『我是位先發投手且投的不錯，我必須去等待一個機會。』 : He has made one appearance in relief for New York, allowing two runs in : a three-inning outing on Friday against the Phillies, and is available : for use beginning on Tuesday. Wang has spoken in a team-first manner, : but the two-time 19-game winner would clearly prefer to be starting. : As of this moment, though, there are no clear-cut opportunities with which : to give him that chance. Girardi said that Hughes will make his next : scheduled start on Sunday against the Indians in Cleveland, which leaves : Wang as a reliever for now. 他已經在New York以中繼身分出賽過一場了，在禮拜五對費城人的比賽投出 3IP / 2Run 的成績，而他還會在禮拜二在出賽。王的言談中表示以球隊優先，但是這位兩度 19 勝 的投手顯然比較喜歡先發。然而截至目前為止，還沒有給他明確的機會。 Girardi說：『Hughes將會按照預定的時間表在下次的比賽先發，上對克里夫蘭印地安人 ，而目前仍會把王建民留在牛棚做為中繼投手。』 : "I think he's somewhat frustrated by it," Girardi said. "It's the way you'd : expect anyone to be if you'd been through what he's been through the last : couple of months. I believe he understands that he's here to help us, and : we feel really strongly that he can be a big part of this club. We need to : get him back to where he needs to be." Girardi說：『我認為王建民會有一點沮喪，如果有人跟王一樣經歷過最近幾個月的事情 他一定也會感到挫折或不舒服的，而讓他扮演中繼投手是一個可以達到我們期待他的方 法，我們相信他(王建民)能夠了解這樣他能夠幫助我們，我們強烈相信他可以成為我們 強大的一員，我們需要他(王建民)能夠恢復到他需要的程度。』 : In an ideal world, the Yankees would have kept Wang on rehab for one more : start and he would have pitched Friday at Pawtucket, but that Minor League : trip was nixed when Joba Chamberlain was hit Thursday by a line drive and : was uncertain to make this next start against the Rangers. : "It was what we needed, and we needed him," Girardi said. "Sometimes things : are done out of necessity. It doesn't always go according to plans and what : you envisioned. We needed him and now that he's here, we still feel that we : need him." : The Yankees are still trying to figure out the best way to make it all : work. 理想上，洋基仍可以讓王建民投一場復健賽先發，而他禮拜五將會在Pawtucket投球，但 是他的小聯盟之旅，在禮拜四Joba被球打到時且不確定他是否能夠在下次對遊騎兵隊時 先發的情況下被喊卡了。 Girardi說：『這是我們需要的，且我們需要他(王建民)，有些時候某些事情是需要去做 的，事情總是不能照你計畫和預期的，我們需要他且現在他也在這，我們仍然覺得我們需 要他。』 洋基球團仍然試著去找出最好的方式去使其都能順利的運作。 : "It's kind of a tricky situation," Girardi said. "You don't want to give him : a lot of work, because that means our starters aren't going long. But we do : want to get him work and we want to get him back to where he was before he : got hurt last year." Girardi說：『這是一個令人棘手的情況，你不想要給他(王建民)很多的工作做，因為這就 表示我們的先發投手不能投較長的局數，但我們確實想要給他投球工作，且我們想要他恢 復到他去年受傷以前的身手。』 : Wang has been told that the Yankees will try to make the situation as normal : as possible, never bringing him in mid-inning and giving him his usual time : to warm up, but the uncertain schedule of relief work has been odd. : "I know what kind of things I should do as a starter," Wang said. : "I don't know what I should do every day [as a reliever]." 王建民已經被告知說，洋基球團將會試著使他(王建民)的狀況盡可能的變的正常，不要 讓他在中間的局數出場投球，給他一個固定的時間去熱身，但是這個不確定出場時間的 中繼投手的安排是臨時的。 王建民說：『我知道我身為一個先發投手每天必須要做的事，但我不知道身為一個中繼 投手每天該做什麼。』 : Girardi said that he finally had seven arms in his bullpen on Tuesday with : Wang's availability and a roster move to replace eighth-inning man Brian : Bruney, who is scheduled to see Dr. James Andrews on Wednesday for another : opinion on his aching right elbow. Girardi說：『在禮拜二時，牛棚加上王建民有7個可用的投手，而在名單中做為在第八局 投球的setup man Brian Bruney，因為的右手痠痛，在禮拜三時被安排去見James Andrews 醫生。』 : Adjusting to the new landscape, the Yankees plan to mix and match the : seventh and eighth innings between Alfredo Aceves, Phil Coke and Jose Veras, : with Aceves viewed as more of a distance option. : New York recalled right-hander David Robertson from Triple-A on Tuesday to : replace Bruney on the roster, but Girardi said that he would probably not use : Robertson in the late innings right away. 為了適應不同的情況，洋基球團計畫在比賽的第7局及第8局由Alfredo Aceves、Phil Coke 及Jose Veras這三位擔任投球的工作，而Aceves會被視為更長局數投球的選擇。洋基把在 3A的右投David Robertson叫上大聯盟，取代原本在牛棚的Bruney，但是Girardi說他在比 賽後面的局數當中可能不會使用牛棚投手Robertson。 : Bryan Hoch is a reporter for MLB.com.
