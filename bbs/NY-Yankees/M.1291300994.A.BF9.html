Derek Jeter Rumors: Thursday By Tim Dierkes [December 2, 2010 at 8:03am CST] Derek Jeter and his agent Casey Close reached out to the Yankees to set up Tuesday's meeting in Tampa, report Mark Feinsand and Bill Madden of the New York Daily News. Hal Steinbrenner, Randy Levine, and Brian Cashman attended for the Yankees. Money and years were not discussed, say Feinsand and Madden. Instead, the meeting concluded with the Yankees' brass asking Close for ideas on how to bridge the large gap. The tenor of the meeting was cordial, reports Michael S. Schmidt of the New York Times. Close did not get back to them on Wednesday. The Yankees were emboldened by Troy Tulowitzki's new deal, in that it has a $15.7MM average salary and Cashman and company offered Jeter $15MM per year.
