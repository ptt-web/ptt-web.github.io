標題[新聞] Martin expected to be Yankees' starting catcher
時間Thu Dec 16 18:26:23 2010
Martin expected to be Yankees' starting catcher Newly-signed Russell Martin is expected to be the Yankees' primary catcher next season, with Francisco Cervelli as the likely backup. Jorge Posada, who has been the Yankees' everyday catcher since 2000, is expected to be the primary designated hitter. The Yankees have two top catching prospects in Jesus Montero and Austin Romine who are expected to get another year of seasoning in the minor leagues. However ,they could also be used as trading chips. Martin, 27, reached a one-year agreement with the Yankees this week. He is a two-time All-Star but he has had declining numbers for a couple seasons. In 2010 he batted .248 with five home runs and 26 RBIs in 97 games, all career lows, and missed two months with a hip injury. He was non-tendered by the Dodgers after the season. Meanwhile, Yankees expect a decision from free-agent pitcher Andy Pettitte soon as to whether he will return for one more season or retire. Martin's old team, the Dodgers, reached an agreement with reliever Matt Guerroer on a three-year, $12 million deal.
