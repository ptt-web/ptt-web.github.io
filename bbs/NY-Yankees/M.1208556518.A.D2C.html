這段是彼得胖部落格上的一篇 我姑且翻一下 Statement from Joba April 18 This statement from Joba Chamberlain, via the Yankees: 從洋基那聽來喬巴的這段話: “After several difficult days, my father is feeling much better. He is still in the critical care unit of the hospital and more tests await him, but he is off the ventilator and breathing on his own. Each day he’s acting more and more like himself, and he’s even giving people grief —myself included — because the hospital doesn’t carry Yankees games on television. "這幾天很難熬，但都過去了。我爸現在感覺好多了。他現在還是在醫院的急重症部門， 而且還有很多檢查等著他。不過他現在已經拔掉呼吸器，可以自己呼吸了。他的身體一 天天好起來，可是他還是常常跟人家(包括我)說他很不蘇湖---因為醫院不給看洋基隊 球賽。" “When things like this occur in life, you certainly take notice of how much your teammates become more like family members. Their unconditional support, along with that of so many fans, has made a very tough time easier to deal with. Everyone’s love has been felt by my entire family, and it has brought great comfort to us when we have needed it most. "遇上了這種事，感覺隊友就像家人一樣。有了他們和球迷無私的支持，就算日子再苦， 也都沒那麼難熬了。在我們最需要幫助的時候，大家的愛讓我們全家都感覺就甘心。" “I look forward to being reunited with my manager, coaches and teammates so I can thank each of them personally for all that they have done for my family.” "很想見見經理、教練、和隊友，謝謝他們這段時間以來對我們家情義相挺。" What we don’t know yet is when Joba will be back but we should get more info on that later today. 不知道喬巴什麼時候回來，不過今天應該就會有比較多消息。 Certainly great news about his dad. 可喜可賀 可喜可賀 --- 可喜可賀 可喜可賀 -- ╴●╴ Lion 統 Ｗ 味 ▼　時 Ｔ 俊 ● 興 Gida Fala MACOTO眼　ｄ　米 ． ．=>． ．一． ．全． ．報． ．國． ．農． ．太． ．雷． ．鏡． ．迪 　︶　 　︶　獅　︶　龍　︶　鷹　︶　熊　︶　牛　︶　陽　︶　公　︶　蛇　︶　亞 ※ ※ 兄　Ｍ　三 ╴[]╴和　Ｗ　中 Luka Agan ￥ 辣 ． ．=>． ．弟． ．商 ． ．信． ．信． ．勇． ．金 angol ． ．妞 　︶　 　︶　象　︶　虎 　︶　鯨　︶　鯨　︶　士　︶　剛 -mois 　︶　熊 -- ※ 發信站: 批踢踢實業坊(ptt.cc) ◆ From: 140.112.250.12
