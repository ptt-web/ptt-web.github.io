標題[新聞] Reports: Yankees like Lowell for first base
時間Fri Nov 16 22:30:37 2007
Reports: Yankees like Lowell for first base ESPN.com news services Even as the Yankees press ahead in their talks to bring third baseman Alex Rodriguez back into the fold, free agent Mike Lowell remains a point of interest. 即使A-Rod即將被洋基簽回來, 洋基對Mike Lowell還是有興趣. If anything, the Yankees are now coming at Lowell from another angle ... or corner. According to numerous media reports, the Yankees are talking with Lowell, Boston's third baseman the last two seasons, about moving from third to first. A television station in Boston, WBZ-TV, said a four-year deal from the Yankees worth $50 million-$55 million is on the table. 根據數個報導, 洋基正在跟Lowell談要把他從三壘移到一壘. 波士頓的電視台WBZ-TV說: 洋基拿出四年50-55百萬的合約來談. "All I can say at this time is that the Yankees have engaged us," one of Lowell's agents, Seth Levinson, told the New York Post. "所有我能說的是:洋基有參與這件事." Lowell的經紀人說. Red Sox general manager Theo Epstein put a new contract for the World Series MVP on the table last week, a three-year offer reportedly worth in the $35 million to $45 million range. 紅襪的GM上週給了世界大賽MVP一個三年35-45百萬的合約. Where the Red Sox were the only team that could negotiate with Lowell through midnight on Monday, others now can make offers. It's not known if the Yankees and Lowell's agents have advanced beyond the discussion phase. 沒有人知道洋基與Lowell的經紀人是否完成討論了. Lowell followed a superlative regular season -- career highs in batting average (.324) and RBIs (120) to go with 21 home runs in 154 games -- with an outstanding October. In Boston's four-game sweep of Colorado, Lowell hit .400 (6-for-15) with four RBIs, three walks and a team-high six runs in winning He homered, doubled and scored twice in the Game 4 clincher at Denver's Coors Field. Lowell本季的成績.................. Lowell, who made $9 million last year, was selected by the Yankees in the 20th round of the 1995 amateur draft. He played eight games for New York in the 1998 season before being dealt to the Florida Marlins in February 1999. He was acquired by Boston from Florida in November 2005 in the Josh Beckett trade. Lowell在1995年業餘選秀第20輪被洋基選到. 他在1998年為洋基打了8場球, 1999年2月被交易到馬林魚. 2005年在Josh Beckett的交易中被換到紅襪. It is likely that Jason Giambi will play out the final season of his Yankees contract as the team's designated hitter. First baseman Andy Phillips is returning in 2008, but Lowell, who turns 34 in February, is known for excellent defense. Giambi在洋基的最後一年可能會擔任DH的角色. 一壘手Andy Phillips在2008年會回來, 不過Lowell以極佳的防守著名.
