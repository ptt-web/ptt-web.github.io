時間Wed Jun 4 10:18:14 2014
標題：Tanaka awarded AL Pitcher of the Month 作者：By Andrew Marchand | ESPNNewYork.com 出處：Espn - http://ppt.cc/Jp7O NEW YORK -- Masahiro Tanaka may have lost his first regular-season start in nearly two years in May, but he was still good enough to win the American League Pitcher of the Month award. 雖然輸了本季第一場比賽，但成績還是太殺，拿到了美聯的五月最佳投手。 Tanaka went 5-1 with a 1.88 ERA in six starts. 6場先發 戰績5勝1敗 1.88 ERA "I'm really honored and happy to be able to receive such an award," Tanaka said through his translator. "But basically, the bottom line is that I'm not pitching or playing to get that award, so I can't really be satisfied because of this." 田中說“我很榮幸可以得到這個獎，但它不是我投球的原因，所以我不會因為這個而 感到滿足” Tanaka's next start is Thursday afternoon against the Oakland A's at Yankee Stadium. Overall, he is 8-1 with a 2.06 ERA. He seems like he will definitely make the All-Star team and could very well start the game. 田中下一場比賽是禮拜四下午對運動家。以今年的成績進明星賽應該是沒什麼問題，甚 至還可能先發呢 On May 20, he lost against the Chicago Cubs at Wrigley Field.
