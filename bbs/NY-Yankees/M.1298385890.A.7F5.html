時間Tue Feb 22 22:44:47 2011
http://tinyurl.com/496lvdg *有錯請指正！ TAMPA, Fla. -- New York Yankees co-chairman Hank Steinbrenner thinks his team's celebration of its 2009 World Series championship may have lasted too long. 洋基老闆Hank Steinbrenner覺得他的球隊慶祝2009年冠軍慶祝的太久了。 "I think, maybe, they celebrated too much last year," Steinbrenner said Monday. "Some of the players, too busy building mansions and doing other things and not concentrating on winning. I have no problem saying that." 「我想他們去年慶祝的太過分了。有些球員忙著去建豪宅或其他有的沒的，沒專注在贏球上 。我講的很清楚了。」 When it was pointed out that Yankees captain Derek Jeter was building a large multimillion-dollar home in Tampa last year, Steinbrenner said he wasn't singling out any individual. 難不成他是在講隊長Jeter—很巧的是Jeter去年才剛在Tampa蓋好豪宅。Hank表示我沒有指 名道姓喔。 "I was just saying, maybe they were riding the wave of '09 a little too much, and it happens sometimes," Steinbrenner said. "This year in spring so far, from what I've seen and what I've been told, they've come in with a real, new drive and determination -- the kind they had in '09." 「我只是說，也許他們太自得於2009年的那座冠軍了，這人之常情啦。今年春天到目前為止 ，就我看到的情況、我發表過的言論，他們現在可認真的--跟09年一模一樣。」 New York lost in the American League Championship Series last season to Texas after fading in the second half of the season and winding up with the AL wild card. 洋基去年下半季丟掉領先地位，以外卡進季後賽，然後在ALCS輸給遊騎兵。 Jeter batted .334 with 18 homers and 66 RBIs for the Yankees in 2009. He slipped to a career-low .270 with 10 homers but did have 67 RBIs last season. The 36-year-old batted .231 in the ALCS. 隊長2009年有16隻全壘打，打擊率.334，但去年只打出.270與10隻全壘打。已經36歲的他在 ALCS更只有.231的打擊率。 After sometimes contentious negotiations -- in which general manager Brian Cashman told him to shop for better offers -- Jeter agreed to a three-year, $51 million contract in the offseason. 今年經過一番唇槍舌戰後—GM Brian Cashman還叫他不如去當FA試試會不會有更好的價碼 --隊長終於同意跟洋基簽下3年51M的新合約。 On Sunday, during his news conference, Jeter said he wanted to shift the focus forward.
