Overall, Banuelos threw 2 scoreless innings, : striking out 3 and allowing a double and a walk. He faced a lineup that : was mostly major leaguers, and did not look overmatched. He threw 28 : pitches, including 20 fastballs, 5 changeups, and 3 curveballs. The : fastball ranged in velocity from 92-96 mph, with the change consistently : in the low 80′s, and the curve in the high 70′s. He was able to throw : all 3 pitches for strikes, and didn’t miss his location very often. : Banuelos did a great job at keeping the ball down, both with his fastball : and the offspeed pitches, which likely contributed to his effectiveness. : I’m no mechanics expert, but his delivery looked smooth and easy as : reported. 昨天春訓對上紅襪的那場比賽讓我有機會第一次在實戰中看到Banuelos 的投球，而他的表現的確讓我印象深刻。帳面上來看，Banuelos投了兩 局無失分、3K、被打1支2B及1BB。面對的大部分是大聯盟級的選手並且 看起來沒有被打垮。所投出的28球中包括20顆快速球、5顆變速球和3顆 曲球，直球球速落在92~96英哩之間，變速球保持在80英哩左右，曲球 則是接近80英哩。他的這三種球路都有辦法三振打者，而且能夠常常投 到想要的位置。Banuelos有辦法使打者不斷打出滾地球，不論是他的直 球或是較慢速的球種，這導致了他投起球來相當有效率。我不是投球機 制的專家，但是我認為他的動作看來很順暢且輕鬆，就如同報告上所說 的。 : One observation is that Banuelos was pretty consistent about the location : of his pitches.
