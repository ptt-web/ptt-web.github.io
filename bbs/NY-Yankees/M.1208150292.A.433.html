標題[新聞] 從紅襪的角度看今天的比賽
時間Mon Apr 14 13:18:09 2008
<節錄> Let the record show that Daisuke Matsuzaka was the winning pitcher. However, even Matsuzaka would probably acknowledge that he was extremely fortunate. Battling with his control all night, Matsuzaka threw 116 pitches over his five taxing innings of work. He gave up five hits, six walks and four runs, striking out two. Nonetheless, Matsuzaka ran his record to 3-0 on a night his ERA jumped to 2.70. On a night the slumping David Ortiz got a rest, the Red Sox led, 7-1, after three innings. 儘管紀錄表示松阪是今晚的勝投投手,然而,即便是松阪自己或許也知道他實在是很幸運. 整晚松阪都在跟他的控球力奮戰,五局的內就花了116球. 被打了5支安打,6次四壞保送, 掉了四分,三振兩次. 但是,松阪的戰績還是變成了三勝零敗,可自責分率跳到了2.70. 今晚,近況不佳的Ortiz休息沒上場,紅襪在三局後就7比1領先了. Yankees starter Phil Hughes -- the phenom of the organization -- looked even more ordinary than Matsuzaka in this one. The right-hander lasted two-plus innings, giving up six hits and seven runs (six earned). Phil Hughes, 洋基的先發投手,眾所期待的明日之星,今晚看來卻比松阪更尋常不出色. 這右投手今晚只撐了兩局多,被打了6支安打,掉了七分(六分自責). Hughes set the tone for the type of game it would be on both sides with a sloppy bottom of the first. After a pair of walks, Manny Ramirez raked an RBI single to right-center. Kevin Youkilis followed with a sacrifice fly to center and Sean Casey added an RBI double to right, and just like that, the Sox had a 3-0 lead. Hughes threw 39 pitches in the inning. Hughes在一局下就為今晚雙方的比賽定調了. 連續兩各四壞保送後... 1. Many Ramirez中右外野安打,1分打點 2. Kevin Youkilis中外野犧牲打,1分打點 3. Sean Casey右外野二壘打,在一分打點 然後紅襪就三比零領先了, Hughes第一局就花了39球... However, this was a night where nothing was easy for either side. Matsuzaka was all over the place in the fourth, throwing 32 pitches.
