時間Wed Dec 21 04:48:38 2011
Free agent starter Roy Oswalt had previously asked for a three-year deal this offseason which rightly scarred off the Yankees, who were probably wondering how a guy goes from contemplating retirement due to a back injury to asking for a long-term commitment. 早先推土機要尋求一份3年合約因此而排除於採購名單之外(傷勢疑慮) Apparently it must have scarred off most teams as the former Astro and Phillie is now looking to sign a one-year deal in an effort to show he is healthy and land a multi-year deal next offseason, according to Jerry Crasnick of ESPN. 如今他似乎改變主意先簽1年短約證明他的健康狀況沒有問題再來要求複數年約 On a one-year deal Oswalt might fit into the Yankees plans. They have been looking to upgrade their rotation, but have found the trade market to be too expensive. Oswalt might be a good alternative as they could sign him for just one season and he wouldn’t effect their long-term payroll plans. 由於覺得今年市埸上的貨都太貴而沒有任何動作,但因為這消息,也許推土機會是個新輪值 的答案 Crasnick said that there are as many as six teams interested in Oswalt on a one-year deal, but he didn't name them.
