時間Fri Nov 16 01:55:13 2007
Sources: Rivera wants 4th year in deal Ken Rosenthal/ FOXSports.com There is a reason why Mariano Rivera has not accepted the Yankees' offer of a three-year, $45-million contract. Mo還沒接受洋基3年4500萬的offer是有原因的. Rivera, according to major-league sources, wants a fourth guaranteed year. Mo想要第四年獲得保證. The Yankees remain the overwhelming favorite to retain Rivera. No other club is expected to even approach their proposal, much less top it. 洋基還是Mo的最愛. But Rivera, who turns 38 on Nov. 29, has instructed his agents to shop him, in part because he was angered by Hank Steinbrenner referring to his age in recent comments about the Yankees' offer, a source says. Mo因為Hank對他的年紀有所評論而生氣, 要經紀人四處兜售自己. Rivera has specifically mentioned following Joe Torre to the Dodgers, and his agents also are contacting other clubs about his availability. Mo說過要跟Torre去道奇, 他的經紀人也試圖跟其他球隊聯繫. Rivera, sources say, believes he deserves the same length of contract as Yankees catcher Jorge Posada, who is on the verge of re-signing for $52.4 million over four years, and Mets closer Billy Wagner, who received $43 million over four years after the 2005 season. Mo相信他值得擁有跟Posada一樣長的四年合約. Posada即將與洋基簽下4年5240萬的合約. Mets的closer在2005年球季結束拿到4300萬四年的合約. Posada and Wagner are both 36, nearly two years younger than Rivera — and Wagner was 34 when he signed with the Mets as a free agent. Posada和Wagner都是36歲, 將近比Mo年輕兩歲. Wagner簽自由球員合約時是34歲. Rivera also is certain to note that third baseman Alex Rodriguez would be 42 at the end of his proposed 10-year contract with the Yankees — the same age that Rivera would be at the conclusion of a four-year deal. Mo一定也注意到A-Rod 42歲時是他10年合約的最後一年. Mo如果簽到四年的合約, 他最後一年也是42歲.
