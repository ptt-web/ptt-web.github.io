Pitching depth on display in McAllister trade The news was a little bit buried in the middle of last night’s game: The Yankees announced that Zach McAllister had been traded to the Indians as the player to be named later in the Austin Kearns deal. 這則消息在昨晚的比賽中有點被掩蓋過去了：洋基宣布McAllister是Kearns交易案中 的那位"PTBNL"。 My first reaction was absolute surprise. That’s a big name to be a PTBNL. McAllister is still just 22 years old and already has a full season of Triple-A under his belt. He had outstanding numbers in Double-A last year, and Baseball America ranked him the Yankees No. 5 prospect this winter. It’s not often a guy like that is traded away as a player to be named. 我第一個反應是超級驚訝。對我來說這個PTBNL有點太大咖了。 McAllister現在才22歲而且已經在3A有過完整個一個球季了。他去年在2A有傑出的一季 ，而BA今年冬天也把他排在洋基新秀的第五名。通常一個被交易走的PTBNL不會是這種咖。 Twelve hours later, it’s less stunning. 過了12小時後，我覺得沒那麼驚訝了。 Outside of spring training, I only saw McAllister pitch first-hand once. He came up for the Triple-A playoffs last year and more than held his own. He was never a guy who seemed to wow anyone with his stuff, but the results spoke for themselves. It wasn’t a fluke that he wound up so high on Baseball America’s list. He’s a better pitcher than this year’s 5.09 ERA in Scranton/Wilkes-Barre suggests. 除今年春訓之外我只親自看過McAllister投球一次。他在去年3A的季後賽出場而且投得 有點掙扎。他從不是個擁有會讓你發出"哇喔"的stuff的投手，但比賽結果會替他說話。 他去年被評為洋基新秀第五名也不是運氣好，他是個比今年的5.09 ERA好的投手。 That said, Ivan Nova clearly moved ahead of McAllister in the Yankees rotation the pecking order. David Phelps, Hector Noesi and Andrew Brackman — probably Adam Warren and maybe Lance Pendleton too — seemed to have passed him among the Yankees upper-level pitching prospects. Manny Banuelos, Dellin Betances and Graham Stoneburner had clearly done the same in the lower levels. 這麼說吧，Nova現在明顯在洋基的順位已經高過McAllister。Phelps、Noesi和Brackman ，可能連Warren，甚至是Pendleton看來都像在洋基前段投手新秀中評價高過他。 Banuelos、Betances跟Stoneburner也明顯在相同層級繳出了跟他當初差不多的成績。 The Yankees needed to protect McAllister from the Rule 5 draft this winter, and given such pitching depth, it might have been tough to find a spot for him on the 40-man. Don’t be surprised if he develops into a legit major league starter, but there’s enough pitching in the Yankees system that they could afford to lose him. 洋基在冬天需要保護McAllister不讓他取得參加R5D的資格，而考量到目前的投手深度， 使得在40人名單抓個位置給他變得有點困難。若他之後成為一個正職先發也不用感到驚訝 ，但目前農場所擁有的投手深度是足夠讓洋基選擇去犧牲掉他的。 -- ※ 發信站: 批踢踢實業坊(ptt.cc) ◆ From: 140.112.217.50
