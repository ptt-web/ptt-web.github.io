I understood and supported the idea of shifting Joba Chamberlain from setup man to starting pitcher. Because, obviously, Chamberlain was still going to be on the team, in a more important role. 1.我了解也支持將Chamberlain從setup man調到SP的決定。因為，很明顯地， Chamberlain將在隊上扮演更重要的角色。 But to replace Chamberlain, they had managed to take Farnsworth _ who had been a complete waste of space for two years _ and turn him into a dominant setup man. Dominant, with that upper-90s fastball and slider. The credit went to Joe Girardi and Dave Eiland. The blame went to Joe Torre and Ron Guidry for what transpired the prior two seasons. 但是為了取代Chamberlain，他們必須容忍Farnsworth，一個曾經佔據名單達2年 而且無用的人，並且使他成為一個具有主宰力的setup man，以他超過90哩的速球和滑球。 榮耀被歸功到了Joe Girard和Dave Eiland。Joe Torre和Ron Guidry則因前2季發生的事 情而被怪罪。 So now they're going to have to rebuild that eighth-inning bridge again, and as good as Girardi and Eiland have proven themselves to be in this area, they'll have no one with the sort of tools that Farnsworth possessed. 所以現在他們得重新搭建一座第8局的橋樑，而且要跟Girardi和Eiland他們在這個領域 已經顯示過的一樣好，但牛棚裡沒有一個人有著Farnsworth所擁有的能力。 2. Pudge. Maybe he can keep up his recent hot streak (42-for-110 with three doubles and four homers, according to the Yankees' press release on this trade). But the prior three years _ including last year, which was a virtual walk year, since the Tigers had a team option on him _ he has been a below-average offensive player. Look at his walk totals since 2005. Brutal. 2.Pudge.或許他能保持著他最近火熱的打擊(110打數42安打，包括3支二壘安打和4隻全 壘打)。但是前三年，包括去年，幾乎沒有保送的一年，從Tigers決定動用球隊選擇權 後，他就成了一名在平均之下的打者。看看他從2005年以來的保送數。令人感到殘酷， 但不得不承認這個事實。 Now, is he an offensive upgrade over Jose Molina? Of course. And defensively, he still nails an impressive share of baserunners (18 of 50, as you can see on his baseball-reference.com page). But was he worth giving up Farnsworth? I don't think so. 現在，他是Molina的攻擊升級版嗎?當然。還有在防守方面，他依舊有著令人印象深刻的 阻殺能力(50次盜壘抓到了18次)。但是值得因為他拋棄Farnsworth嗎?我不這樣認為。 3. The Dave Dombrowski factor. Here are the three major trades that Cashman has made with Dombrowski, prior to today: 3.Dave Dombrowski這個因素。下面是三次Cashman在今天前和他做的三筆大交易： 1) February 1, 1999. Mike Lowell to the Marlins for Mark Johnson, Todd Noel and Ed Yarnall. 1999/2/1 Mike Lowell <=> Mark Johnson , Todd Noel 和 Ed Yarnall 2) July 5, 2002. Jason Arnold, John-Ford Griffin and Ted Lilly to Oakland; received Jeff Weaver from Detroit, as part of a three-way deal. 2002/75 Jason Arnold , John-Ford Griffin 和 Ted Lilly <=> Jeff Weaver 3) November 10, 2006: Gary Sheffield to Detroit for Anthony Claggett, Humberto Sanchez and Kevin Whelan. 2006/11/10 Gary Sheffield <=> Anthony Claggett Humberto Sanchez 和 Kevin Whelan The first two were horrendous _ and those were Cashman's deals. They weren't pushed ahead by George Steinbrenner, or Tampa people, or anyone else. The last one, well, the Yankees benefited by disposing of Sheff. And the Tigers are worse for having him. So far, the trio of young pitchers the Yankees received hasn't panned out, although, to be fair Sanchez needs another year to be at full strength after his 2007 Tommy John surgery. 前面2個令人畏懼，而這就是Cashman所做的交易。他們不會被大老闆Steinbrenner、 Tampa的人們以致任何人所贊同。最後一項，洋基從揮棄Sheff中獲得了好處，Tigers 也因為有了他而變差。但截至目前為止，洋基所得到的2名年輕投手都尚未投出成績， 雖然Sanchez在2007年接受Tommy John手術後，仍需要一年的時間復健。 So this is a case of buyer beware. Dombrowski and Jim Leyland are as sharp a GM-manager duo as there is in the major leagues. What does it tell you about Pudge that the Tigers were willing to trade him, despite his respectable statistics? 所以這就是一個買家需要注意的地方。Dombrowski和Jim Leyland就像任何一個大聯盟裡 精明的GM一樣。儘管Pudge有著可觀的數據，但什麼原因會讓Tigers願意交易他? 4. Oh, and forget about offering Pudge arbitration and getting draft picks out of it. He's making $13 million this year. If the Yankees offered him arbitration, he'd get a raise from that, whereas he'll absolutely have to take a pay cut when he goes into the open market.
