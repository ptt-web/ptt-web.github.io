時間Fri Jul 25 07:18:12 2014
標題：Yanks land lefty Capuano from Rockies 來源：洋基官網 NEW YORK -- The Yankees continued to stay busy on the transaction front Thursday, acquiring left-hander Chris Capuano from the Rockies in exchange for cash considerations. 用現金跟Rockies換到左投手Chris Capuano。 Capuano, 35, was signed as a free agent by the Rockies on July 4 and made four starts between Double-A Tulsa and Triple-A Colorado Springs, posting a 1-0 record with a 2.79 ERA. 35歲的Capuano在7月4號被Rockies簽下，然後在2A跟3A有 1-0 2.79 ERA的成績。 Earlier this year, the veteran lefty went 1-1 with a 4.55 ERA in 28 relief appearances for the Red Sox before being released. 之前是紅襪後援，有 1-1 4.55 ERA 28後援出賽的成績。 Capuano has a career record of 74-84 with a 4.28 ERA in 266 games (209 starts) over 10 Major League seasons with the D-backs (2003), Brewers (2004-07, '10), Mets (2011), Dodgers (2012-13) and Red Sox (2014). Yankees general manager Brian Cashman has said that the team is actively looking to upgrade the roster, both significantly as well as with smaller incremental transactions. Already this month, New York has acquired right-hander Brandon McCarthy, left-hander Jeff Francis, infielder Chase Headley and now Capuano, while releasing outfielder Alfonso Soriano. 繼Soriano被release之後，持續的補強了McCarthy，Francis，Headley跟Capuano。 ----------------------------------------- 算是小小補強吧 這位撿來當先發備胎？ 文章也有提到田中的狀況，說治療10天了 右手肘還是有不舒服。 但洋基還是有信心他今年回得來... 田中會有大概3個星期的時間不能投球， 之後也要面對投牛棚跟小聯盟復健賽的考驗。 受傷的Teixeira則有可能進DL，洋基已經讓他休息了幾天看看是怎麼樣的狀況， 可能這幾天就會決定吧。 -- ※ 發信站: 批踢踢實業坊(ptt.cc), 來自: 58.96.158.184 ※ 文章網址: http://www.ptt.cc/bbs/NY-Yankees/M.1406243894.A.E54.html
