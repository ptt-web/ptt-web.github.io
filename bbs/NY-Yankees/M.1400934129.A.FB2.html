標題：Notes: Brian Roberts X-rays negative 作者：By Andrew Marchand | ESPNNewYork.com 日期：May 24, 1:04 AM ET 原文：http://ppt.cc/ef5A 文章後面有提到Jeter跟Johnson。 ------------------------------- CHICAGO -- New York Yankees second baseman Brian Roberts said X-rays on his bruised right knee were negative. He plans on being available for Saturday's game after fouling a pitch directly off his kneecap. Roberts今天比賽自打球打中膝蓋，右膝經過檢查後沒有問題，明天的比賽預計可以上場。 "Everything is fine," Roberts said following the Yankees' 6-5 loss to the White Sox. After fouling the ball off the knee in the fourth inning, Roberts remained in the game until being taken out for defense in the ninth. Roberts雖然第四局的時候自打球，但是他繼續留在場上直到第九局因為守備原因被換 下場。 "I just said, 'I don't want to be a detriment,' " Roberts said of what he told manager Joe Girardi. "If you think someone can move around better, then feel free. I told him I could finish." Roberts告訴Girardi他不想拖累球隊，但他今天可以戰鬥到最後。 -----分格線----- Jeter on Aparicio: Derek Jeter tied Luis Aparicio for second place on the all-time games played list at shortstop. Jeter追平Luis Aparicio 並列史上游擊出賽第二多的選手。 "Aparicio, everyone knows how great he is," Jeter said of the shortstop who played the majority of his career with the White Sox. "I guess it is ironic that we are here. It is hard to believe when you think about the history of the game. There is only one guy who has played more games. It is something I'm proud of. I take pride in doing my job and being available to play every day. You have your name in the company with someone like him, it is pretty special." Jeter說他很榮幸能夠達成這個成就，畢竟以歷史來說只有一個人在游擊出賽更多場比賽。 而大家都知道Aparicio （生涯大部分剛好都在白襪）是一個偉大的球員，自己能與他相 提並論是很特別的。 Johnson will play first again: Even after his struggles on defense at first, Girardi said he will play Kelly Johnson there again. Girardi說會繼續試著讓Johnson守一壘，即便今天守備沒有很好。 "He is a guy who can handle that position, I believe," Girardi said. "Tonight, he had a tough night. They weren't the easiest plays in the world. One was scoop, the other one was a low throw into the runner a little bit. He can handle it."
