標題[新聞] West: Rivals' slow play 'embarrassing'
時間Fri Apr 9 10:11:51 2010
http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/news/story?id=5066650 If you're struggling to stifle yawns and stay focused the morning after watching the New York Yankees and the Boston Red Sox play another 4-hour marathon, umpire Joe West feels your pain. Before Wednesday night's game, West called the AL East rivals "embarrassing" and "a disgrace to baseball" for slowing the pace of their games to a crawl, according to the Bergen (N.J.) Record. The teams' season opener on Sunday took 3:46 to play. Tuesday night's game went 3:48, while Wednesday night's contest, which went 10 innings, was finished in a relatively brisk 3:21. "They're the two clubs that don't try to pick up the pace," said West, the chief of the umpiring crew working the three-game series, according to the report.
