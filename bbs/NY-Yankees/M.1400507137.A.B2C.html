時間Mon May 19 21:45:35 2014
標題：CC: Captain Crutch leaves rotation hobbling 作者：By Wallace Matthews | ESPNNewYork.com 出處: ESPN - http://ppt.cc/d~MR 新手試翻 （取大概的重點 有誤請多包涵） 文章前面在講CC 後面在講今天對Pirates的比賽 只試翻CC近況 --------------------------------------------- NEW YORK -- The New York Yankees don't know exactly what is wrong with CC Sabathia's right knee, or exactly what course of treatment will be necessary to relieve his pain. They don't know if the inflammation that caused the knee to swell with fluid in his past two starts was the reason for his early season ineffectiveness, or if he will be better, worse or pretty much the same pitcher when he finally does return to the Yankees' rotation. 洋基不知道CC右膝發生什麼事，或著他的膝蓋的問題是造成他前兩場先發悲劇的原因。 洋基也不知道他康復回來後會變成怎樣的投手（更強，更悲劇，或跟現在一樣） But they do know this: Sabathia will not be in uniform when he is eligible to come off the disabled list a week from Monday. 但他們知道一件事：CC在下禮拜一是趕不及回到球場上。 "It's not going to be a 15-day DL, it's going to be longer, I can tell you that," manager Joe Girardi said after the Yankees split a doubleheader Sunday with the Pittsburgh Pirates, winning the first game 4-3, behind Hiroki Kuroda, and losing the nightcap 5-3 when Alfredo Aceves gave up a seventh-inning home run to Josh Harrison that proved to be the game-winner. Girardi在今天的雙重賽後說：他可以告訴你，CC可能要花比15天DL還要更久的時間。 Girardi said Sabathia -- who had just returned from a visit to Dr. James Andrews' clinic in Pensacola, Fla., where he received stem-cell treatment via injection -- would be examined by team doctor Chris Ahmad on Monday at New York Presbyterian Hospital. CC禮拜一會繼續做檢查，他已經去給Dr. Andrews檢查過了，並接受了stem cell的治療。 "The doctor will examine him and they’re going to try to get a game plan for how he moves forward from here," Girardi said. "When we’ll get him back I don’t think any of us really know, because this is not something that's done very often." Girardi說：他不知道CC什麼時候會回來。 The Yankees have said Sabathia has "degenerative changes" in his right knee, the same one that underwent surgery for a meniscus tear after the 2010 season. Other than that, Girardi couldn't provide many specifics. He didn't know if Sabathia was likely to have in-season surgery, an almost certain season-ender, or if he would need prolonged treatment and rest followed by a spring training-like period of buildup before he could return to the rotation. 洋基說CC的右膝有退化性的改變，跟2010年後開刀治療的meniscus tear一樣 Girardi不確定他會不會動手術（動的話CC這季也就結束），也不確定他是否需要長久 的治療，修養及復健。 He cited the differences in Michael Pineda's injury, characterized as a "right shoulder muscle injury" in the area of the latissimus dorsi, and Sabathia's injury, which appears to be soreness, inflammation and fluid buildup of unspecified origin. "You look at Michael’s injury, there’s a lot of data behind that one to give you an idea it’s going to be about six weeks," Girardi said. "But on CC’s, there’s not." 不像Pineda你很確定Pineda受了什麼傷，但CC目前受的傷不明確 只知道有痠痛 發炎及 積水在不確定的地方。 你知道Pineda的傷需要大概6周的康復時間，但CC的傷你完全不知道。 Sabathia made a brief appearance at the Stadium during the first game Sunday, had some treatment and left, on crutches, without speaking to the media. So it was impossible to gauge his level of pain, frustration, or encouragement -- or discouragement -- over the injury, or to get his opinion on how long he expected to be out.
