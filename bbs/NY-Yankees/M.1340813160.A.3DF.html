時間Thu Jun 28 00:05:57 2012
CC Sabathia placed on DL with groin strain Yankees placed LHP CC Sabathia on the 15-day disabled list, retroactive to June 25, with a left groin strain. Sabathia was diagnosed with a Grade 1 strain of his left adductor muscle. He suffered the injury in the fourth inning of Sunday's start and told the team following a bullpen session Tuesday. The good news is that it's the least severe strain and the Yankees expect the durable left-hander to return right after the All-Star break. Freddy Garcia will take his place in the rotation and start Friday night against the White Sox.
