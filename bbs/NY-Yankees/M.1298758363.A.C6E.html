標題[新聞] Yankees looking at Francisco Liriano
時間Sun Feb 27 06:12:39 2011
According to Bob Nightengale USA Today, the New York Yankees are keeping a close eye on left handed pitcher Francisco Liriano. Nightengale adds that the Twins are keeping tabs on the Yankees' prospects just incase the Yankees press the panic button on their rotation and call about Liriano. The 27-year-old can become a free agent after the 2012 season and with a solid 2010 season Liriano has revamped his trade value from when he made only 14 starts due to injuries in 2008 and pitched to a 5.80 era in 2009.
