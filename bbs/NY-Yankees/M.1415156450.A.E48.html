標題[外電] Alfonso Soriano Announces Retirement
時間Wed Nov 5 11:00:47 2014
http://www.mlbtraderumors.com/2014/11/alfonso-soriano-announces-retirement.html Longtime big leaguer Alfonso Soriano has annunced his retirement from the game , reports Hector Gomez of Dominican outlet Listin Diario (via Twitter). The 38-year-old played in parts of 16 MLB seasons, including thirteen as a full-time regular. 在聯盟打滾了16年的Soriano宣布退休了 Soriano was once one of the most consistent power threats in the game. Between 2002 and 2013, Soriano averaged 624 trips to the plate per season, slashing .273/.324/.511. He hit 385 home runs (32 per year) and stole 243 bags (20 a season) over that stretch. 在2002到2013年間 Soriano平均有624個打席且繳出.273/.324/.511的成績 生涯打出了385支全壘打以及243次盜壘 Soriano appeared in seven straight All Star games (2002-08). While generally subpar defensive marks hurt his overall value, Soriano was ultimately worth just under 40 fWAR in his career, though he checked in at less than 30 rWAR. 在2002~2008連續七年入選明星賽 不過因為在平均以下的防守成績 使他的整體價值下降 生涯通算fWAR在40以下 The Dominican native started and ended his career in pinstripes, joining the Yankees after an early-career stint in Japan. He was later dealt to the Rangers and then on to the Nationals, where he moved from second to the outfield, and ultimately signed an eight-year, $136MM deal with the Cubs. Soriano美國生涯的開始以及結束都在洋基 從日本廣島鯉魚隊(參考wiki) 接著就來到了洋基 接著被洋基交易到遊騎兵後再轉到了國民以及小熊 守備位置也從二壘轉到了外野 最後在小熊那邊簽了8年136M的合約 That massive contract had its ups and downs, but Soriano ultimately swung an above average bat in every year except for a rough 2009. He spent the tail end of the deal back in New York after a deadline deal, delivering an excellent stretch of play late in 2013. But he struggled to get it going last year, and was ultimately cut loose by the Yanks in mid-season. 在合約期間 除了2009年以外 他都可以繳出平均以上的打擊成績 最後在2013年下半季再度回到了洋基 但是因為2014的掙扎表現 因此在季中被洋基給裁掉 --------------------------------------------------------------------- 英文不好 如果有翻譯錯請指正 只是大概翻譯一下 主要只是要告訴大家這個難過的消息 雖然生涯大部份時間都待在小熊 但是還是洋基相當有代表性的球員 可惜去年的contact實在太差了 完全傷害球隊 只好忍痛解約了 果然歲月不饒人還是得走上退休的道路 謝謝他對洋基的貢獻 -- ※ 發信站: 批踢踢實業坊(ptt.cc), 來自: 101.11.91.183 ※ 文章網址: http://www.ptt.cc/bbs/NY-Yankees/M.1415156450.A.E48.html
