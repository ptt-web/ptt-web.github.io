時間Tue Aug 21 00:41:26 2007
Edwar Ramirez: Selling the Changeup 小拉米 變速球強力放送中 I don't care who you are, what your mechanics look like and what pitches you throw. Check out Edwar Ramirez's stats this year in the minors. Here's the link to his stats for his minor league career. 不管你是何方神聖，你有多強，或是你投什麼球路， 都得來看看今年小拉米在小聯盟有多變態.... Let me give you a brief rundown of his statistical performance: 簡單看一下他的數據表現: 2007: 56 2/3 IP, 26 H, 22 BB, 102 K's, 0.79 ERA...over 16 K's/9 between AA and AAA 2006: 56 IP, 93 K's, ridiculous numbers (indy ball and High-A) After a successful debut where he struck out the side, he got lit up (after a long time between appearances) in the appearance before he got sent down last time. Now that he's back up in the bigs, I thought the timing was right for this piece. 他用三振對手來達成他成功的大聯盟初登板，而他自己當時也顯得很興奮， 然而卻在這之後不久後，他被送回小聯盟 不過，現在他又回來了，我認為是時候了... Ordinarily, you would expect someone who puts up strikeout numbers like this to have an overpowering fastball and some nasty slider/splitter kinda pitch. Well, Edwar Ramirez doesn't have an overpowering fastball or a killer slider. What he does throw is one of the best changeups in all of baseball. Not only does it have good movement and speed differential from his fastball, but what he does best is that he "sells" his changeup. 通常人們期望看到的是，用具有強力壓制性的快速球，以及一些噁心到極點的滑球 或快速指叉球來三振對手，小拉米雖然沒有這些武器，他丟的僅僅就是變速球， 不過丟的卻是最好的那種. 相較他的快速球，他的變速球有很好的尾勁跟速度， 而且他做的最好的一點，就是對於他的變速球，總是不吝惜的強力放送....
