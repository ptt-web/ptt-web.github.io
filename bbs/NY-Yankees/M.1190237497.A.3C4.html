Banged-up Giambi sits vs. Orioles By PETER ABRAHAM THE JOURNAL NEWS (Original publication: September 19, 2007) 一，本來昨天托瑞是準備讓大吉先發當一壘手的，最後大吉還是沒先發， 因為他身上現在有兩個小傷：一個是痛痛的右腳，另個是腫腫的右肘。 二，痛痛的右腳，是星期一金鶯的 Cabrera一記銳利的滑球直接砸到大吉 右腳前端。（為躲這球大吉還跌倒了哩，結果還是沒躲過....囧rz。） 腫腫的右肘，是上星期六在紅襪被 Beckett賞了個報復性觸身球，直接 打在右肘。 三，X 光檢查後還好，只是黑青。但別忘了，大吉六月左腳曾有大傷呀.... 四，托瑞說大吉還是可以代打，但得休個一兩天。 五，托瑞預計，如果今天大吉可以上場，就要讓大門休假一天。因為季賽只 剩下11天，托瑞希望他的先發球員在允許的狀況下能有足夠的休息。 （但，今天先發的DH不是大吉，是血力‧瞪啃......所以，大吉傷勢有 點令人小擔心哩。） 補最新消息： UPDATE, 6:04 p.m.: Jason Giambi’s right foot is still sore and he was sent to Columbia Presbyterian Hospital this afternoon for further tests. No word yet on the results. 大吉右腳持續疼痛，被送去醫院作進一步檢查了 難怪今天沒上場當DH... ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ NEW YORK - Yankees manager Joe Torre planned to start Jason Giambi at first base against the Orioles last night. But Giambi sat out a second straight game because of two injuries: a sore right foot and a swollen right elbow. Baltimore's Daniel Cabrera hit Giambi on the top of the foot with a slider in the second inning of Monday night's game. Giambi took a Josh Beckett fastball in his elbow on Saturday in Boston. "He could pinch hit but as far as anything more than that, he's a day or so away," Torre said. "He's gotten beat up a little." X-rays taken on Monday night revealed only a bruise. Giambi was relieved the ball hit him on the right foot. It was his left foot that was surgically repaired in June. Doug Mientkiewicz started his third consecutive game at first base and was 2 for 4 with four RBI in a 12-0 victory. If Giambi can at least DH tonight, Torre plans to give Johnny Damon a day off. With only 11 games remaining in the regular season, Torre wants to give his regulars a rest when he is able.
