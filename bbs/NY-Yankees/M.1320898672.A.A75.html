時間Thu Nov 10 12:17:50 2011
Posada: “It’s not going to happen” http://goo.gl/8GJDc Jorge Posada can read the writing on the wall as well as anyone. Brian Cashman has not yet reached out to Posada’s agents this offseason, but Posada doesn’t need a phone call to tell him the Yankees don’t have a spot for him on their roster. “I don’t think there’s even a percentage of a chance that I can come back, ” Posada said. “… It’s not going to happen.” Posada said five or six teams have already contacted him this offseason, and he started working out on November 1 — which he’s always done — but at this point he’s trying to decide between retirement and playing for a new team. Cashman還沒有跟Posada的經紀人接觸過 不過 並不需要一通電話來告訴他洋基沒有他的位子 "我不認為自己有任何可能會回洋基，這不可能發生" Posada說 有五六支球隊跟他接觸過 不過他還沒決定是要退休還是在其他球隊打球 “I will always be a Yankee,” he said. “The Yankees for me is my second family. It would be tough to put on another uniform for real and learn another set of rules and all that stuff, but that’s one of those things. I have to see if I want to keep playing… Do I want to do it for somebody else? Do I want to leave home? Do I want to do it all over again without knowing anybody? It would be tough. I’ve got great people, great friends and great teammates and it would be tough to learn new people again.” "我永遠是洋基人，洋基就是我第二個家" Posada說 "穿上其他球衣、學習其他規則以及所有東西是很艱難的，不過這只是其中之一 如果我想繼續打，我必須問我自己：我要為了其他人而戰嗎？我要離開家嗎？ 我要在不了解其他人情況下從頭來過嗎？這會很艱難。 我已經有很多好友、很多好隊友，重新了解新朋友會很困難的事。" Posada said he’s not upset with the Yankees. He understands what’s happening, and he understands why it’s happening. He called his relationship with the organization, “a great partnership” and said the first check that came into his foundation for tonight’s dinner was from the team. Posada說他對洋基不感到沮喪，他了解發生什麼事，他也知道為什麼 他說他跟洋基之間是"良好的夥伴關係" 而且他的第一筆薪水就是從這球隊來的 “At the end of the day, it’s a business,” Posada said. “You look back and you wish there was some things that could have gone differently, but they didn ’t. There’s nothing I could control. Everything happened for a reason. I’m not bitter at the Yankees. I’m not bitter at Joe Girardi. I’m not bitter at Brian Cashman. It just happened.” "到頭來，這就是生意" Posada說 "你回頭看，期待有些事會不同，不過事與願違 沒有事是我可以控制的，任何事會發生都有理由 我不是在挖苦洋基，我沒有挖苦Girardi，我也沒有挖苦Cashman 它就是發生了" Posada said he’s been talking a lot with Bernie Williams — talked to him today, as a matter of fact — and Bernie has told him to make sure his decision is the right one. Posada’s wife, Laura, said she’s told her husband to make sure he doesn’t look back with any regrets (about either staying or going). Posada guessed that he won’t make up his mind until closer to February. Posada說他跟Bernie聊了很多 Bernie告訴他：確定你做的決定是對的 Posada的老婆則跟他講：無論做了什麼決定都不要後悔 Posada覺得直到二月前他不會做決定 “If I don’t want to play, you guys will be the first ones to know,” Posada said. “I’m not one of those guys that’s going to linger around and wait.
