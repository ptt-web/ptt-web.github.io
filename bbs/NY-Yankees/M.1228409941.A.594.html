Yanks want Pettitte back and to take pay cut 洋基希望派帥降價回來 The pitching-needy New York Yankees want to re-sign Andy Pettitte, and Pettitte reiterated Wednesday that he wanted to return, but the two sides are at a stalemate over his salary, according to a report in the New York Times. 根據一篇New York Times的報導指出，極需投手的紐約洋基隊想要把Andy Pettitte簽回 來，而Pettitte星期三重申他仍想要為洋基投球，但是雙方無法對他的薪資達成共識。 "I mean, to tell you the truth, like I've told you all a hundred times, I've made it loud and clear where I'd like to play at," Pettitte, 36, said in a telephone interview. "I'm just basically sitting here letting my agents do their job really." 年屆36歲的Pettitte在一場電話訪談中說："我是說真的，我的意思就像我曾告訴你們大 家無數次那樣，我已經很清楚明白的公諸於世：我想為洋基投球。" "我基本上就只是坐 在這裡等著我的經紀人做好他的工作而已。" What Randy and Alan Hendricks, Pettitte's agents, are trying to do is to make sure their client avoids a pay cut. Pettitte made $16 million last season, one of the highest salaries in the major leagues for a pitcher, and his agents have stressed that he wants the same salary. The Yankees have offered Pettitte $10 million, and they seemingly have no immediate plans to improve their offer. Pettitte的經紀人Randy 及 Alan Hendricks試著在做的就是確認他們的當事人拒絕降價 。Pettitte上個球季拿到1600萬，這是一份MLB投手的頂級合約之一，而他的經紀人強調 他想要一樣的價格。洋基卻只想給Pettitte 1000萬，而且他們近期似乎也沒有提高價格 的打算。 "We'd like to have him back," General Manager Brian Cashman said Wednesday. "It's come to the arena of trying to achieve common ground on money, which is easier said than done." 洋基GM 現金人星期三表示："我們當然想找他回來。" "現在就只是無法在「錢」的意見 上達成共識，「錢」之一字說是很簡單，要付就很難。" Pettitte battled shoulder problems last season and ended up 14-14 with a 4.54 earned run average, including a 2-7 mark to finish the year. The Yankees believe Pettitte, who pitched a team-high 204 innings, can still be an effective starter, but they consider a $16 million investment in him to be too expensive.
