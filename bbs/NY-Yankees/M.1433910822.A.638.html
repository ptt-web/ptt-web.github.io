標題[外電] Yankees sign Sergio Santos
時間Wed Jun 10 12:33:38 2015
http://bronxbaseballdaily.com/2015/06/yankees-sign-sergio-santos-to-a-minor-league-deal/ According to Ken Rosenthal of FOX Sports, the Yankees have signed reliever Sergio Santos to a minor league deal. The Yankees will send Santos to Double-A Trenton where he’ll tryout for the team with hopes of eventually joining the Yankees bullpen. In 12 games with the Los Angeles Dodgers this season, Santos had a 4.73 ERA and a 1.500 WHIP.
