標題[新聞] A-Rod tabs Oseary for guidance (翻譯)
時間Wed Dec 19 15:53:38 2007
NEW YORK -- Alex Rodriguez may be staying in the Big Apple, but that doesn't mean he can't have a taste of Hollywood. A-Rod雖然仍呆在紐約, 但這不代表他不能去試試看好萊塢 The Yankees third baseman has latched on with personal manager Guy Oseary to handle his ventures outside of baseball, though he continues to retain Scott Boras -- with whom he is not currently on speaking terms -- as his agent. 這位洋基的三壘手聘請了Guy Oseary來處理他棒球之外的事業, 儘管他繼續保留了他最 近都不太提起的Scott Boras當他的經紀人. Variety reported late Monday that Rodriguez, 32, had signed an agreement with Oseary, who also manages Madonna, Lenny Kravitz and magician David Blaine. 上周一有很多報導都指出32歲的A-Rod, 已跟旗下有著Madonna, Lenny Kravitz跟魔術師 David Blaine的Oseary簽署了份同意書 "As I embark on this new chapter in my career, I know that I have found in Guy Oseary someone who is aligned with my interests and who has earned a lot of respect in the entertainment industry," Rodriguez said in a statement. “當我展開我事業的新章節時, 我知道Guy Oseary就是我要找的, 一個跟我興趣相契合, 也在演藝圈有著足夠聲望的人.”A-Rod在一份說明李這樣說著. Oseary, a former record company executive, is also a partner in Untitled Entertainment, whose star clients include Hilary Swank, Penelope Cruz, Naomi Watts and Ashton Kutcher. A-Rod will be Oseary's first baseball client. Oceary, 曾擔任過某唱片公司執行長, 現在則在擁有Hilary Swank希拉蕊史旺, Penelope Cruz潘尼洛普克魯茲, Naomi Watts納歐密華茲以及Ashton Kutcher的 Untitled娛樂公司擔任合夥人. A-Rod將會是他第一個棒球方面的客戶. "He's focusing on baseball and needs someone whose interests are aligned," Oseary told Variety. "This is to help him have more control of his image and brand." Oseary跟媒體說明, “A-Rod現在專注在棒球上面, 也需要有興趣契合的人來幫他, 這將 會幫助他對於自己的形象及品牌有更多的掌控. A spokesperson for the Major League Baseball Players Association confirmed that Rodriguez continues to retain Boras as his agent for baseball-related matters. 一位大聯盟球員協會的發言人證實了A-Rod繼續保留Boras來當他在棒球相關方面的經紀人. However, in an interview aired Sunday, Rodriguez told CBS' "60 Minutes" that he is not currently speaking with Boras, who has represented the three-time All -Star since he was a teenager. 然而, 在一份周日發表的面談中, A-Rod跟CBS 60分鐘節目說, 他最近都沒跟這位在他還是 青少年時曾代表著三次全明星賽的Boras聯絡了 "The whole situation saddens me a little bit," Rodriguez said. "There hasn't been much back-and-forth talking." A-Rod說, “這全部的狀況讓我感到難過, 有太多來來回回的對談了” The rift resulted after Boras leaked word of Rodriguez's contractual opt-out during Game 4 of the World Series, leaving Rodriguez -- in his word s -- "white as a ghost" while watching reports from his Florida home. 當A-Rod在佛羅里達家中得知, Boras在世界大賽第四戰透漏了A-Rod決定使用契約上的選擇 權逃脫的時候, 他說, “我簡直嚇到臉色發白”, 於是他跟Boras之間便產生了裂痕. Though Rodriguez would reconcile with the Yankees, finalizing a 10-year, $275 million deal earlier this month, Boras was not involved in the negotiations. Rodriguez consulted with Omaha, Neb.-based billionaire Warren Buffett, a personal friend, and leaned upon his wife, Cynthia, during the process. 儘管A-Rod還是跟洋基和解了, 在這各月初簽署了一份10年價值275萬美金的合約, 但Boras並沒有參與這次的會談. A-Rod請教了他私人的朋友華倫巴菲特, 也仰賴了巴菲特 的妻子來進行這次的會談. Oseary said that he does not have immediate plans for A-Rod's foray into the entertainment industry, but said that he will consider all possibilities. Oseary表示, 對於A-Rod突然要進入演藝圈的事情他還沒有立即的計畫, 但他會考慮所有的 可能性. "Obviously, there are a tremendous amount of opportunities for Alex," Oseary said. "It's about him having someone who he respects who can look at endless possibilities. I don't look at any one area -- and these are areas he has not explored." 很明顯的, 對A-Rod來說有太多的機會了, 對他來說是去擁有個他尊敬的,可以看到無限 機會的人. 我並沒有限定特定的領域, 而這些都是他之前沒接觸過的領域. -- ※ 發信站: 批踢踢實業坊(ptt.cc) ◆ From: 60.250.135.187 ※ 編輯: djviva 來自: 60.250.135.187 (12/19 15:54)
