標題[新聞] Yankees re-sign Pettitte to one-year deal
時間Thu Dec 10 00:31:10 2009
http://tinyurl.com/yl5ghpd The Yankees have re-signed Andy Pettitte for one-year at $11.75 million, the Post has learned. The two sides quickly hammered out an agreement once Pettitte signaled he did want to play again in 2010 -- and only for the Yankees. Between base salary ($5.5 million) and bonuses, Pettitte earned $10.5 million in 2009. So the Yankee rationale was that Pettitte should get that plus a minor raise considering the strong season he had last year. The Yankee offseason plan was built around obtaining Curtis Granderson and re-signing Pettitte, and both might be officially announced today.
