from http://tinyurl.com/l7256t Alex Rodriguez drives in four as Yankees outlast Braves in slugfest 靠著阿肉的四分打點洋基在這場激烈的球賽中擊敗勇士 ATLANTA - After watching Alex Rodriguez swing the bat Thursday night, Joe Girardi may never rest him again. 今晚看了阿肉的表現後, 小喬應該不會再讓阿肉下去休息了吧 A-Rod had his best game in a month, helping the Yankees to an 11-7 win over the Braves to send them home for the Subway Series feeling pretty good about themselves. 阿肉今晚的表現是本月最棒的一場, 幫助洋基以11-7擊敗勇士隊, 也讓大家開開心心的 準備回家打地鐵大戰 Rodriguez went 3-for-5 with a home run, a walk and four RBI, collecting his first multi-hit game since he went 5-for-5 against the Rangers on May 25 - also the last time he drove in four RBI in a game. 阿肉今晚五支三的表現, 包含了一支全壘打, 一次保送以及四打點, 是從5/25對遊騎兵 的5支5後, 睽違已久的單場多支安打表現, 5/25的這場阿肉也是單場四分打點 That Rodriguez was in the lineup at all came as a bit of surprise, since Girardi had said on Sunday that he would rest him once during the Braves series, part of a new plan to get him one game off per week leading up to the All-Star break in order to keep him fresh. 今晚的阿肉完全是個大驚奇, 本來小喬上周日還想讓阿肉在跟勇士的系列賽中再休息一 次, 一週休息一場之類的直到明星賽, 為了確保他能完全復原 With three high-profile games against the Mets this weekend, it seemed like a foregone conclusion that he would sit out Thursday night's finale in Atlanta. That wasn't the case, as the Yankees - apparently with the blessing of team doctor Chris Ahmad and hip surgeon Dr. Marc Philippon - are prepared to play the third baseman all three games this weekend against the Mets despite starting him each of the past three nights. 接下來是備受矚目的地鐵大戰, 本來今晚對勇士系列賽的最後一場, 阿肉應該是在旁邊 看球到結束而不上場. 但阿肉還是上場了, 因為有得到隊醫們的同意. 再接下來的三場 地鐵大戰中, 隊醫們也準備好了隨身照料阿肉 "They said he's at the point where the feel he can play five or six days in a row," Girardi said. "Alex feels good, he feels like he's got a lot of energy in his legs. We'll see how he feels tomorrow and check with him on Saturday and Sunday. If he feels good, we'll let him keep going, because we need his bat in the lineup." 小喬說隊醫表示阿肉可以連續上場個五天六天, 沒問題. 阿肉也說他感到自己狀況良好, 雙腿充滿力量, 明天的比賽再看看他的狀況, 然後有需要的話六日再來調整. 如果他還是 感到很順, 我們就會讓他繼續上場, 因為打線很需要他的火力 That was certainly the case Thursday night. A-Rod homered against Derek Lowe in his first at-bat, then singled in another run in the second inning to help the Yankees build a 3-1 lead. After Johnny Damon's bases-loaded triple boosted that lead to five, Rodriguez reached on an error to start the fourth, later scoring on Cody Ransom's two-run double that stretched the lead to 8-1. 阿肉第一個打席就尻全壘打, 第二局一分打點安打幫助洋基3比1領先, 第三局大門在滿 壘時三壘安打把領先提升到5分後, 第四局一開始阿肉靠著失誤上壘, 再靠著Ransom的兩 分打點二壘打回來得分, 此時比數來到8-1領先 Rodriguez's home run was his second away from Yankee Stadium all season, the first coming on his first swing of the year in Baltimore on May 8, snapping a 69-at-bat homerless streak on the road - his longest since 1997. It was also career home run No. 563 for A-Rod, tying Reggie Jackson for 11th place on the all-time list. 今晚阿肉的全壘打是本季離開洋基主場後的第二支, 第一支是5/8對金鶯擊出的, 之間 相隔了69個打席, 是阿肉自1997以來最長的紀錄, 今晚這支也是阿肉生涯的第563號, 讓阿肉在歷史排名上跟Riggie Jackson並列第11位 Andy Pettitte was unable to take advantage of the commanding lead, giving up five runs in the bottom of the fourth with the help of a pop single that fell between Damon, Brett Gardner and Derek Jeter and another dropped fly ball by Gardner. Pettitte lasted only 3 2/3 innings, charged with six runs (three earned) in the no-decision. 派大並沒從這樣的火力中受益多少, 因為他在四局下一口氣失掉五分, 主要是一支掉落在 大門, Jetter跟Gardner中間的德州安打, 還有一顆Gardner沒接好的飛球. 派大只撐了 3又2/3局, 六失分裡三分責失, 無關勝負(no decision) With the lead still at two runs in the seventh, Jeter reached on an infield single and Damon walked, and after a double-steal, Mark Teixeira was intentionally walked to load the bases. A-Rod made the Braves pay for that decision, unleashing a two-run single that pushed the lead back to four. 七局一開始洋基還領先兩分, Jeter先是靠著場內安打上壘, 大門獲得保送, 鐵爺獲得 刻意保送(敬遠), 這時阿肉生氣了, 尻出一支兩分打點安打讓勇士為敬遠鐵爺而選擇來 對付阿肉付出了代價 There is no guarantee that A-Rod will play all three games against the Mets, as both he and Girardi said they would approach it on a day-by-day basis. But with a scheduled day off on Monday, it's a safe bet that Rodriguez will play all weekend. "In a perfect world, we can play all (three) and then rest on Monday," Rodriguez said. "We have to follow Philippon's program, there's no question. This week doesn't fall into that because of the natural days off and the days off in Florida. The idea is one day off a week." 阿肉會不會在地鐵大戰中三場都出賽還不確定, 阿肉跟小喬都表示一天一天的來看狀況 再說. 但因為週一休賽, 所以讓阿肉三場都上看來沒啥問題. 最棒的可能就是三場阿肉 都上, 然後週一剛好一起休息, 毫無疑問的我們得照著Philippon的時間表出賽.這一週 沒這樣是因為剛好沒有公休日 我們的想法是一週休一天 A-Rod was mired in a 1-for-25 slump when he knocked in a pair of runs with a bases-loaded single on Wednesday, giving the Yankees a lead they would never relinquish. He drew a pair of walks after that, then built on it with his big game last night, finishing off his most successful two-night stretch in what has been a brutal month for him. 昨晚阿肉本來還陷在25打數僅一支安打的低潮裡, 昨晚他在滿壘時擊出兩分打點的安打 讓洋基獲得領先, 之後阿肉獲得了兩次保送, 讓昨晚的比賽變成自己的Big Game. 靠著 這兩場的精采表現, 結束了他這個月以來打擊不振的難堪 "I'm going to go out and do the best I can every night," said Rodriguez, who raised his average from .210 to .223. "Sometimes it's a couple walks and one hit; it doesn't have to be a 500-foot home run. It just has to be good at-bats, situational hitting and play good defense. I do have my limitations." 阿肉說, 以後的每場比賽我會走出來然後表現出最好的自己, 今晚他的打擊率已經從 .210 -> .223, 往往都是幾次保送後來個一支安打, 沒有必要都來個500英呎的全壘打, 只需要上場打擊時好好打就夠了, 依每次的狀況來打擊, 然後好好防守, 我也只是人. -- ※ 發信站: 批踢踢實業坊(ptt.cc) ◆ From: 60.250.135.187
