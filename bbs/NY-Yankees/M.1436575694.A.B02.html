標題[洋基] Refsnyder headed to the bronx
時間Sat Jul 11 08:48:11 2015
http://goo.gl/ES0JHp BOSTON - Yankees fans who have been clamoring to see Rob Refsnyder, the club's top infield prospect, the last two years apparently will get their wish. With the Red Sox scheduled to throw lefty starters Saturday and Sunday, the righthanded hitting second baseman will be called up Saturday morning, according to the YES Network. Refsnyder, 24, impressed scouts throughout the spring with his bat but not so much with his glove and his minor league season has played out as such. Refsnyder entered Friday with a .290/.387/.413 slash line with seven homers and 37 RBIs in 81 games but with a team-high 13 errors, though he's committed just two of those since May 23. Refsnyder is 14 for his last 34 in his last 10 games, including three straight multi-hit games. He was selected by the Yankees in the fifth round of the 2012 draft out of the University of Arizona.
