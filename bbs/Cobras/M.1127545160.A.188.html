Long time no see!!! Kleber sent me this picture of him trainning in Ibiuna, a city near Sao Paulo. It has been almost a month he started to pitch hard but he is still doing his rehab as usual. I am very proud he has been putting all his effort onto this everyday. He cant wait to go back to Taiwan! It will still take a couple of months, so please be patient and keep praying for his recovery! Thanks for the support and visit us here sometime!
