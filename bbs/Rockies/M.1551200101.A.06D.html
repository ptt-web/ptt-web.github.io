標題[情報] 洛磯開出年均35M的七年約給納豆
時間Wed Feb 27 00:54:57 2019
根據 Ken Rosenthal 的透露 洛磯開給 Arenado 的展延合約的價碼是 260M/8yr， 據傳 Arenado 已經同意了這個合約 ============================================================================= https://www.mlb.com/news/nolan-arenado-extension-with-rockies Arenado, Rox near record extension, source says By Matt Kelly The Rockies and their third baseman Nolan Arenado are finalizing an eight-year contract worth $260 million, according to a report by MLB Network insider Ken Rosenthal. The deal was first reported by ESPN’s Jeff Passan, and a source tells Passan that the extension includes an opt-out clause after its third season -- which would allow Arenado to hit the free-agent market at age 30 -- along with a full no-trade clause. 根據 Ken Rosenthal 的消息來源，我們三壘手 Arenado 正在完成8年260M的大約，這個 消息最初是由 ESPN 的記者 Jeff Passan 所揭露，合約內容包括 Arenado 可以在合約 執行三年後，也就是在他 30 歲的這一年選擇跳脫合約投入自由市場，且包含了完整的 不得交易條款。 The club has not confirmed the extension, which would keep its superstar in Denver through 2026 and give Arenado the highest average annual salary in history. 球隊還沒有正式承認這個可以將陣中明星 Arenado 留至2026年的史無前例野手年均薪 水最高的合約。 The deal would pay Arenado an average annual salary of $32.5 million, which surpasses the $31 million average annual salary benchmark set by Miguel Cabrera’s eight-year, $248 million extension with the Tigers from 2014 and the three-year, $93 million deal that Dodgers ace Clayton Kershaw signed in November. 這筆合約中，Arenado 將可拿到年均薪 32.5M，正式超過 Miguel Cabrera 在 2014 年 與老虎簽下的 248M/8yr ，以及 Clayton Kershaw 於11月與道奇簽下的 93M/3yr 所保 持的年均薪31M記錄。(編按:最高記錄為 Zack Greinke 的年均 34.4M 的6年約) The Rockies originally agreed to terms with Arenado in January on a $26 million salary for 2019 to avoid arbitration (a record for any arbitration-eligible player), and this new deal would replace that contract beginning this season. Arenado would have led next offseason’s free-agent class if the two sides were unable to agree on an extension. 原先因避免薪資仲裁，於一月與 Arenado 所簽的 26M/1yr 合約，將被這個新約所取代。 倘若 Arenado 沒有同意這筆展延合約，他將成為今年季末最炙手可熱的自由球員。 Arenado, 27, has led the Rockies to back-to-back postseason appearances while already becoming one of the most beloved and accomplished players in franchise history. He has averaged 40 home runs and 126 RBIs over his past four seasons -- all of them being All-Star campaigns -- while winning a Gold Glove Award at third base in each of his six Major League seasons. Arenado has also finished within the top five in National League MVP voting in each of the last three years. Arenado already ranks within the Rockies’ top 10 players all-time in most major offensive categories. 27歲的 Arenado 帶領球隊首次連兩年打進季後賽，是隊史上最受歡迎打得最好的球星之 一。過去四季中，他繳出了平均40HR/126RBI的恐怖成績，這四年都打進了明星賽。而且 ，從他的菜鳥球季開始，他就是國聯三壘金手套的六連霸得主。此外，近三年他都在年度 MVP的票選中拿到了前五名。儘管還年輕，他已經在洛磯隊史許多攻擊數據中佔據前10名 的位置。 -- ※ 發信站: 批踢踢實業坊(ptt.cc), 來自: 124.218.41.40 ※ 文章網址: https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/Rockies/M.1551200101.A.06D.html
