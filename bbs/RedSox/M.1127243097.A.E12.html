Theme Song: None officially, but several unofficial theme songs exist: - played in the middle of the eighth inning at Fenway Park: Neil Diamond's "Sweet Caroline", performed with raucous audience participation. - played after each victory at Fenway Park: "Dirty Water" by The Standells. - played after Dirty Water and for rallies: The Dropkick Murphys' rewrite of Tessie. The original Tessie, a Broadway tune, was adopted by the Boston fans during the 1903 World Series and sung regularly until 1916. - played during David Ortiz's at-bats: "Who's Your Papi?".
