標題[外絮] CJ McCollum: 我絕對不會加入勇士隊的
時間Thu Aug 16 09:25:17 2018
來源: Paradise Post 網址: https://tinyurl.com/y9sxmt4f Blazers guard CJ McCollum says he’ll never join the Warriors CJ McCollum: 我絕對不會加入勇士隊的 Portland Trailblazers guard CJ McCollum has had a lot to say about the Warriors this summer. 阿拓後衛CJ McCollum對於勇士隊真的很有話。 On Wednesday, a video surfaced of McCollum talking to CCTV in China about the Warriors’ recent moves in free agency. In the clip, said the notion of joining the Warriors, “disgusting” and pushed back when the commentator floated the idea that free agents will flock to the Bay Area. 這星期三，一支McCollum在中國央視節目受訪的影片曝光，講到勇士隊最近在FA的動作。 在這段短片，在提到"加入勇士"這個話題，他用了"噁心"表示他的意見，並且不同意主持 人說FA們會群聚到勇士隊這樣的可能性。 影片: https://tinyurl.com/y9asjzda “I think some players will take that route,” McCollum said. “But most guys have too much pride, want to really win on their own or in their certain organizations, and aren’t going to just jump the bandwagon.” 「我認為一些球員會選擇那樣的方式，」McCollum說。「但是大多數球員都有很高的自 尊，會希望自己帶隊拿或是待在他們自己的隊伍，而不會就是抱團的。」 McCollum doubled down on his disdain when asked if he’d ever join the Warriors. McCollum當被問到會不會加入勇士隊時，他對那表示不屑。 “I’m not built like those guys,” McCollum said. “I was raised differently. ” 「我不是那樣的人，」McCollum說。「我被帶大的方式不是那樣。」 McCollum’s comments are just his latest tiff with the defending champs. Last month, the Blazers guard got into a back and forth on Twitter with Kevin Durant. After Durant appeared on McCollum’s podcast, the Blazers’ guard responded to a tweet asking if Durant should be called out of his name. McCollum這次的評論只是他最近的一次對衛冕軍的另一次鬥嘴。上個月，他才在推特上跟 KD戰的難分難解。在KD出現在McCollum的podcast後，他在推特上回應了有人問他說，KD用 B****罵他這件事。 McCollum responded, tweeting: “I still think the b word is harsh and shouldn ’t be used. . He know that decision was soft but I respect it. It’s like getting jumped with ur brothers by a gang you should’ve beat then joining the gang that jumped u and ur brothers 2 months later & forgetting about your bros.” McCollum在推特上說，「我始終認為B****這個字很髒，不是應該用的字。KD知道那個決定 是軟弱的，但是我尊重他。不過那就像是跟你的兄弟被別團修理，你應該要打敗他們，而 不是兩個月之後加入修理你跟你的兄弟那團，然後忘記你的兄弟。」 Durant didn’t take too kindly to the tweet, saying, “So,I would get into a gang fight, lose, plot on my brother for 2 months in our home and then go get the gang we lost to and beat him up? U think that low of me CJ?” KD對那樣的推特很不爽，說，「所以，我是那種打群架打輸了，然後就躲在家裡兩個月， 計畫設計我的兄弟，然後就加入對手，好打敗我的兄弟? CJ，你認為我有那麼低級嗎?」 Considering the brewing hostility between McCollum and the Warriors, its safe to assume the Blazers’ first visit to Oracle Arena Nov. 23 will be must-see television. 考慮到McCollum跟勇士之間逐漸升高的敵意，11/23阿拓到舊金山這場比賽實在不得不 看。 -- ※ 發信站: 批踢踢實業坊(ptt.cc), 來自: 61.222.1.181 ※ 文章網址: https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/NBA/M.1534382722.A.5B0.html
