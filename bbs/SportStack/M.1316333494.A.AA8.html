一年又過去了ˊˇˋ 去年的Stack UP我去畢旅沒參加 今年是11/17 http://www.thewssa.com/stackup/2011_stackup/ 內文： The 2011 WSSA STACK UP! November 17th Help set a new Guinness World Record for "Most People Sport Stacking at Multiple Locations in One Day." The number to beat is 316,736! Now's the time to register to help set a new Guinness World Record™ How? Put together a group of 25 or more stackers who will each stack at least 30 minutes on Guinness World Records Day, November 17, 2011. The Details Online registration AND verification! Much easier to sign up your school/organization and verify your participation on the day of the event. Last year, many schools stocked up food pantries as they stacked up, involved Senior Citizens in their day and took pledges to benefit local charities. The WSSA encourages you to STACK UP! for a good cause that has meaning to you, then use this event as a springboard to benefit your community. *Important Note: Guinness World Records requires that all schools and organizations must verify 25 stackers or more to register, participate and be counted.
