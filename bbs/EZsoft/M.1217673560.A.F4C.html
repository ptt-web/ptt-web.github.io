標題[情報] TotalCommander 7.04 release
時間Sat Aug 2 18:39:18 2008
What's new? The most important new feature is support for ZIP encryption, both the old ZIP 2.0 method, and the new AES (advanced encryption standard) encryption. Both are compatible with common zip packers. There are also many small fixes and improvements to existing functions. A list of additions and corrections, also for previous versions, can be found here. ( http://www.ghisler.com/history.txt ) As usual, the update is free for all registered users. Note: The update notification will be sent to all users on our info mailing list during the next few days.
