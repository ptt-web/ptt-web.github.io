時間Fri Apr 26 16:03:22 2019
※ [本文轉錄自 NBA 看板 #1Smhhgj2 ] 作者: fw2009tc (銅人Sib) 看板: NBA 標題: [外絮] 自15-16球季以來 小李長射命中數僅次咖哩 時間: Fri Apr 26 16:01:42 2019 外電來源:http://0rz.tw/ZDg2x 第一次嘗試翻外電 如果有錯誤還請多指正 感謝~ Damian Lillard trails only Curry on 3s from 30 feet since 2015-16 自2015-16賽季以來，Damian Lillard在30呎外的三分命中數僅次於Curry While Portland Trail Blazers guard Damian Lillard may have surprised many onlookers when he sized up Oklahoma City Thunder forward Paul George and stepped back to 37 feet, it was the shot he wanted. 當波特蘭拓荒者隊的後衛Damian Lillard面對奧克拉荷馬雷霆隊前鋒Paul George使用 後撤步移動到37呎處出手時，許多觀眾可能會感到驚訝。但對Lillard來說，這球是他 心中所想的那個出手。 As a series-winning attempt, it was a bold choice — to say the least. But he had the hot hand and the shot connected, pushing the Trail Blazers into the second round. Many would second-guess an attempt from so far beyond the three-point line, especially considering Lillard took the shot clock to the last second to allow no rebuttal. 作為一個系列賽的致勝關鍵出手，這是一個大膽的選擇--至少可以這麼說。但Lillard 的火燙手感與出手命中，將拓荒者推進到了第二輪。許多人可能會對這一個距離三分線如 此之遠的出手提出質疑，尤其是考慮到Lillard還刻意將進攻時間留到了最後一秒，好讓 雷霆完全沒有還手的機會。 During the postgame press conference, George had some negative things to say about the final shot in the series. 在賽後的記者會上，對於該系列賽的最後一擊，PG說了一些負面的言論。 “Yeah, that’s a bad shot. I don’t care what anyone says. That’s a bad shot. But he made it. That story won’t be told, that it was bad shot. You have to live with it.” "嘿對，那是個糟糕的出手，我不管其他人怎麼想，那就是個糟糕的出手。但他投進了， 故事會流傳下去。但我還是覺得那是糟糕的出手，就是這樣。" But it was a look that Lillard has grown accustomed to over the past few years, which led to the confidence he showed to even let it fly. 但對Lillard來說，這球只是過去幾年習以為常的出手。這也體現在他對於該球所呈現 的信心上。 http://0rz.tw/jB3W9 Including the postseason, he has made 37 shots from at least 30 feet since 2015-16. Golden State Warriors superstar Stephen Curry is the only player who has taken and made more in this time. 算上季後賽，自從2015-16賽季以來，Lillard過去總共投進了37顆超過30呎的三分球。 在這幾年中，只有金洲勇士的超級巨星Stephen Curry的進球數比他多。 Lillard trailed just Atlanta Hawks rookie Trae Young this season but led all players in the Western Conference in total makes on shots from this deep. 而在本季，Lillard也僅落後於亞特蘭大老鷹的新秀Trae Young，並成為西區中投進最多 這種長射的球員。 However, these long looks have accounted for just 6.2 percent of his total three-point attempts over the past three seasons. For comparison, the Atlanta rookie has taken 14.5 percent of his three-pointers from at least 30 feet away from the basket. That means he isn’t relying on them but rather utilizing them when possible. 儘管如此，在過去三季裡，這類的長出手只佔了他全部三分出手的6.2%。作為比較， Trae Young在至少30呎的長射，佔了他三分出手的14.5%。這也顯示了對Lillard來說， 他並不是仰賴這種長射作為主要武器，而是在可運用時最大化它們的效果。 https://imgur.com/j2c2je7.jpg
▲自16-17球季以來，各球員在超過30呎的三分總命中數 (譯註：這邊圖上寫的是自16-17球季以來，但文章中說的都是自15-16球季，我想應該是 圖片打錯了，特此說明。) Lillard has had little trouble connecting on these incredibly difficult three-pointers. Over the past three seasons, he is shooting 37-for-122 (30.3 percent) from ridiculously long range. Lillard在命中這種高難度三分上沒什麼問題。過去三季裡，在這種令人超乎想像 的深遠距離，他總共122投37中(命中率30.3%) That ranks better than Curry (27.8 percent) despite similar volume as well as Kyrie Irving (25.6 percent), Chris Paul (24.3 percent) and quite a bit better than James Harden (15.8 percent). 儘管數量上相近，但這樣的命中率紀錄優於Curry(27.8%)，也優於Kyrie Irving(25.6%)、 Chris Paul(24.3%)，更比James Harden(15.8%)來的好上許多。 This year, however, he is a ridiculous 20-for-51 (39.2 percent) from between 30 feet and 40 feet. For comparison, the NBA average for three-pointers was 35.5 percent this season. 然而在今年，介於30呎到40呎的出手中，Lillard是恐怖的51投20中(命中率39.2%)。做為 比較，本季全NBA的三分平均命中率也才35.5%而已。 In fact, the Portland guard is currently 8-for-12 (66.7 percent) from at least 30 feet during the postseason. The most recent look, of course, was by far the most exciting. But it was not exactly something out of the blue for Lillard. 實際上，在今年的季後賽中，這位拓荒者後衛在超過30呎的長射中是12投8中。最近命中 的一顆，當然就是最令人興奮的那一顆。但對Damian Lillard來說，這真的不是什麼令他 出乎意料的事。 http://0rz.tw/H9Csh https://imgur.com/hHzG4Ko.jpg
