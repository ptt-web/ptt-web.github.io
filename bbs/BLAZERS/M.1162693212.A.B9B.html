http://behindblazersbeat.blogs.oregonlive.com/default.asp?item=269995 Przybilla out a week Trail Blazers center Joel Przybilla will miss at least the next week because of a lower abdominal bruise suffered during Friday's game at Golden State. That's the official terminology ... what really happened is Przybilla got kneed in the groin (you do the math) while blocking a shot in the first quarter of the Blazers' 102-89 loss. It's particularly bad news for the Blazers because reserve center Raef LaFrentz has already been declared out for at least a week, and possibly two, because of his strained calf. So what do the Blazers do at center? Coach Nate McMillan said Jamaal Magloire will start at center, and power forward Zach Randolph will have to slide over and play center when Magloire goes to the bench. As a result, a domino effect will take place - Travis Outlaw will play more power forward to cover for Randolph, and Stephen Graham could see some time at the four as well, McMillan said. The Przybilla injury will definitely keep him out of the next three games _ Minnesota tonight, at the Clippers on Monday and the Lakers on Wednesday. He might be able to play in next Friday's game against New Orleans, however. Time will tell.
