標題Mailbag: Lowry on trading block?
時間Fri May 18 14:55:31 2007
With the press to get Tim Lincecum up here, the unlikelihood of any of the current starters to move to the bullpen and the premium on starting pitching, are we going to see a midseason trade involving Noah Lowry? Is he on the block? -- Rory H., Oakland I believe the Giants would indeed consider trading Lowry, but only for a legitimate offensive threat. General manager Brian Sabean wasn't overwhelmed by any of the offers he received for Lowry or any of the Giants' other young, promising pitchers during the offseason. In the wake of the Francisco Liriano trade, Sabean won't part with a fairly promising, 26-year-old left-handed starter such as Lowry without some definite return. You probably heard the rumors about Lowry going to the Yankees for Melky Cabrera. The Giants would want somebody more proven and productive than Cabrera -- more like Miguel Cabrera of the Marlins, who was the subject of several covetous letters to the mailbag.
