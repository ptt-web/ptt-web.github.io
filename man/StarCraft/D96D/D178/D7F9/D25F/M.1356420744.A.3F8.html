http://us.battle.net/sc2/en/blog/1172226#blog 美版官網的新聞，皮卡丘之前的冠軍官網在報導了XD The second ESL EU female gaming tournament exhibited some awesome StarCraft II gameplay from a talented group of ladies. Please join us in congratulating Taiwan’s Linda “Pikachu” Liao of Taiwan for emerging victorious from a group of 31 of the best female players in North America and the EU. Not only is Ms. Liao a skilled StarCraft II player, but she is also well-known in Taiwan as a talented singer, actress, and television personality! Pikachu didn’t let any of that distract her as she guided her protoss armies in a commanding performance to clear the field of opponents. She finally met Jana “Dzejna” Vyroubalová of the Czech Republic in the deciding best-of-three series. Despite getting caught off guard by Dzejna's proxy pylon assault in game one, Pikachu went on to decisively take the other two games in the series. Liao will go down in history as the first female player from Taiwan to win an international StarCraft II tournament! You can learn more about the competitors at the ESL female gaming site and the StarCraft II Cups SC II goesFemale#2 Tournament tree.
