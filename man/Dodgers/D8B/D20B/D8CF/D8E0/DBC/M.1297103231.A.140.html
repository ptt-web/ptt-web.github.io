Scott Podsednik turns down option with Dodgers Scott Podsednik has opted for free agency instead of exercising his half of a mutual option for 2011, but that doesn't necessarily mean he won't be playing for the Dodgers next season. "We're still engaged in discussions with the Dodgers," said Ryan Gleichowski, the outfielder's agent. Podsednik is presumably asking for more than $2 million, the value of the option he declined. The Dodgers exercised their half of the option Tuesday.
